Description

StudyBuddies is a web application to help students find others to study with and to develop study habits. Sometimes students don't know others in some of the classes that they're taking. StudyBuddies aims to remedy this by allowing people to connect, meet and study. Students can add classes they're taking to automatically search for study groups. if no study groups are available, students can create one.

Duration

August 2015 - September 2015

Team Members

Me, Christopher Lo, Vania Lei, Christy Wong

Role

Project Overview

This web application was designed for a course at UCSD. The first half of the course involved designing logos and prototyping. Logos and prototypes were made using Adobe Phototype and Justinmind, an interactive wireframe. During this time, our team came up with various needs, such as having a simple and user friendly interface, as well as a convenient way of allowing students to find study buddies.
I held the role of UI/UX designer and developer. Using high-fidelity prototypes and Bootstrap, I was able to bring the application to life.
